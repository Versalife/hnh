Rephrasing ideas in the precise language of mathematics allows questions to be asked and
answered in more useful, quantitative ways. ... A bird's eye view of the landscape is a
valuable perspective. It allows the observer to discover unexpected connections between
different parts. However, in order to make those connections precise and rigorous, one
requires the frog's attention to detail. One need to, as the frog does, frolic in the mud.
