About the Name

Why Hubris+Humility? - To do any meaningful work *you have to first believe that you can* do meaningful work, work that is field defining. This requires a certain degree of self confidence — a belief in one’s ability to excellently do such work. This is Hubris. On its own, this belief is not enough. It is a necessary but ultimately insufficient. - However, when coupled with the ability to recognize one’s limitations in skill and the ability to patiently, and systematically remedy those deficiencies .. this is humility.
